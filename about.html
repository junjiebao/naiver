Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our yacht manufacturing and build process

Where are NAVIER YACHTS vessels built?

Our core production shipyard is in Longkou, Shandong, China — a full-scale facility with aluminum fabrication lines, assembly workshops, and quality testing. Design, engineering, and client project management are led from our Dubai Design Center.

Do you offer OEM and white-label manufacturing?

Yes. We provide contract manufacturing services for international marine brands. Clients supply specifications; we handle full production, quality assurance, and delivery from our Longkou shipyard.

What types of vessels can you build?

Our shipyard constructs aluminum catamarans (18ft–60ft+), monohull patrol vessels, commercial workboats, tourism vessels, and custom one-off projects. We work from client designs or provide full concept-to-delivery engineering.

How does the build process work?

1) Initial consultation and specification review, 2) Design & engineering (Dubai), 3) Production planning & material procurement, 4) Hull fabrication & assembly (Longkou), 5) Outfitting, systems integration & QC, 6) Sea trials & certification, 7) Shipping & delivery worldwide.
