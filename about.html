Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about becoming a NAVIER YACHTS dealer

How do I apply to become a NAVIER YACHTS dealer?

Simply complete the dealership application form on this page. Our partnership team will review your application within 2-3 business days and contact you to discuss the next steps, including territory availability and partnership terms.

What are the financial requirements for dealership?

Requirements vary by market and territory. Generally, we look for established businesses with strong financial standing, good credit history, and sufficient working capital to manage inventory, showroom operations, and marketing activities.

Do you offer exclusive territories?

Yes, qualified dealers may receive exclusive territorial rights based on market size, dealer capabilities, and performance commitments. Territory exclusivity is determined during the application review process.

What types of boats and yachts will I have access to?

As an authorized dealer, you'll have access to our complete product lineup including luxury yachts (18FT, 28FT, 32FT models), recreational boats, fishing vessels, and commercial security boats. New models and exclusive editions are also available to our dealer network.

What training and support do dealers receive?

We provide comprehensive training including product knowledge, sales techniques, service and maintenance procedures, and business management. Ongoing support includes marketing assistance, lead generation, technical support, and regular business reviews.

How long does the dealership application process take?

The initial application review typically takes 2-3 business days. For qualified applicants, the complete process including interviews, territory discussions, and partnership agreement usually takes 2-4 weeks depending on due diligence requirements.
