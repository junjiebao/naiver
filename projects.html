TideMaster Cat 38 - Dubai Luxury Catamaran Completed

November 5, 2025 Yacht Projects NAVIER YACHTS Dubai, UAE

Dubai, UAE — NAVIER YACHTS is proud to announce the successful completion of our flagship TideMaster Cat 38 luxury catamaran. This state-of-the-art vessel represents the pinnacle of our engineering excellence and is now ready for delivery to its Dubai-based client.

Engineering Excellence

The TideMaster Cat 38 combines cutting-edge marine technology with luxurious comfort. Every aspect of this 38-foot catamaran has been meticulously designed and crafted to deliver exceptional performance, stability, and comfort in the Arabian Gulf waters.

Key Features and Specifications

  • Length Overall: 38 feet (11.6 meters)
  • Beam: Wide catamaran design for maximum stability
  • Hull Construction: Marine-grade aluminum for durability and performance
  • Propulsion: Twin outboard engines for superior maneuverability
  • Fuel Capacity: Extended range for long-distance cruising
  • Accommodation: Luxury cabin with modern amenities
  • Navigation Systems: Latest GPS, radar, and fish-finding equipment
  • Safety Features: Comprehensive safety equipment exceeding international standards

Performance Characteristics

The catamaran hull design provides exceptional stability in various sea conditions, making it ideal for both leisure cruising and fishing expeditions. The wide beam ensures comfortable living space while maintaining excellent performance characteristics.

Luxury and Comfort

The interior features premium finishes, spacious seating areas, and modern galley facilities. Large windows provide panoramic views and excellent natural lighting throughout the cabin.

Project Information

Completion Date: November 5, 2025

Destination: Dubai, UAE

Vessel Type: TideMaster Cat 38 Luxury Catamaran

Client: Private Dubai Owner
