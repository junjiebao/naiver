TideMaster — Custom Build Series

Aluminum catamarans engineered in Dubai, manufactured in our Longkou shipyard. Every vessel built to client specification.

Built for Performance & Durability

Each TideMaster is custom-constructed using marine-grade 5083 aluminum, with hull design optimized in Dubai and precision fabrication at our China production base. Built for fishing charters, dive operations, tourism, and private recreational use.

  • Marine-grade 5083 aluminum hull construction
  • CNC-precision fabrication at Longkou shipyard
  • Custom deck layout & configuration options
  • Dubai-engineered hull design for stability & efficiency
